## 🔥 THE BIG 3 (Huge Features)

### 1. 💰 Finance & Crypto Dashboard
Track the top 6 cryptocurrencies in real-time!

**What it does:**
- Shows live prices for Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, Solana, Cardano, and Ripple
- Updates every 2 minutes automatically
- See 24-hour price changes (green = up, red = down)
- Market capitalization for each coin
- Powered by CoinGecko API (completely free!)

**How to use:**
- Just scroll down to the "Finanzen & Crypto" section
- Click the refresh button to manually update
- No configuration needed!

---

### 2. ✅ Habit Tracker with Streaks
Build better habits and track your progress!

**What it does:**
- Create unlimited habits (exercise, reading, meditation, etc.)
- Track completion for each day
- See your current streak and longest streak
- Total completions counter
- 7-day calendar view
- Choose from 12 icons and 8 colors

**How to use:**
1. Click "Habit hinzufügen" button
2. Enter habit name (e.g., "Morning Workout")
3. Add description (e.g., "30 minutes exercise")
4. Pick an icon and color
5. Click "Hinzufügen"
6. Every day, click "Heute erledigen" to complete it!

**Pro Tips:**
- Click on any day in the week view to toggle it
- Your longest streak is your personal record
- Habits are saved locally - no account needed!

---

### 3. 🎵 Music Player
Your personal music player right in the dashboard!

**What it does:**
- Play/Pause/Skip tracks
- 5-track demo playlist
- Progress bar with time display
- Animated album art
- Click any song to play it

**How to use:**
- Click the play button to start music
- Use skip forward/back buttons to change tracks
- Click on any song in the playlist to jump to it
- The disc spins when music is playing! ✨

**Note:** This is a demo player. You can connect it to Spotify API in the future!

## 🛠️ THE QUICK 5 (Smaller but Powerful Tools)

### 4. 💬 Quote of the Day
**Start your day with inspiration!**
- New quote every day
- Automatically changes at midnight
- Saved locally

### 5. 🧮 Calculator
**Quick calculations without leaving the dashboard!**
- All basic operations (+, -, ×, ÷)
- Clear button
- Real-time calculation
- Perfect for quick math

**How to use:**
- Click numbers and operators
- Press "=" for result
- "C" to clear

### 6. 🎂 Birthday Reminders
**Never forget a birthday again!**
- Shows birthdays in next 30 days
- Countdown to each birthday
- Add unlimited birthdays

**How to use:**
1. Click "Geburtstag hinzufügen"
2. Enter name and date
3. Done! It'll show up when it's near

### 7. 👟 Step Counter
**Track your daily activity!**
- Set goal (default: 10,000 steps)
- Visual progress ring
- Quick-add buttons (+500, +1000)
- Resets daily automatically

**How to use:**
- Use the +500 or +1000 buttons to add steps
- Or manually track your steps throughout the day
- Resets automatically at midnight

### 8. 💱 Currency Converter
**Convert between 5 major currencies instantly!**
- EUR, USD, GBP, JPY, CHF
- Real-time conversion
- Swap button to reverse

**How to use:**
1. Enter amount
2. Select "from" currency
3. Select "to" currency
4. Conversion happens instantly!
5. Use swap button to reverse

## 🚀 Best Practices

### For Habits:
- Start with 2-3 habits, don't overwhelm yourself
- Check off habits same time every day
- Celebrate your streaks!

### For Crypto:
- Check prices regularly but don't obsess
- Use it for information only
- Remember: crypto is volatile!

### For Step Counter:
- Update throughout the day
- 10,000 steps is roughly 5 miles
- Use quick-add buttons for convenience

### For Birthdays:
- Add them when you think of them
- Check the dashboard weekly
- It shows birthdays up to 30 days in advance
